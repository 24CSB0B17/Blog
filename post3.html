‘Ferrari One of a Kind’ exhibition on Personalisation opens at Museo Enzo Ferrari

Updated on January 13, 2024 at 07:19 AM IST

The year-long display celebrates the craftsmanship behind each unique masterpiece that emerges from the Ferrari factory gates thanks to the exclusive personalisation programme offered by the company’s three Tailor Made centres in Maranello, New York and Shanghai. Now open to the public, the exhibition reveals the exclusivity of these one-of-a-kind cars, each expressing the personality, taste and desires of individual Ferrari owners.

The new “Ferrari One of a Kind” exhibition at the Museo Enzo Ferrari in Modena was officially inaugurated. The display celebrates the craftsmanship behind each unique masterpiece that emerges from the Ferrari factory gates thanks to the exclusive personalisation programme offered by the company’s three Tailor Made centres in Maranello, New York and Shanghai. Now open to the public, the exhibition reveals the exclusivity of these one-of-a-kind cars, each expressing the personality, taste and desires of individual Ferrari owners.

In line with tradition, the “One of a Kind” exhibition at the Museo Enzo Ferrari inModena opened its doors to the public on February 18 in celebration of Enzo Ferrari’s birthday, and will run until February 17, 2025.
